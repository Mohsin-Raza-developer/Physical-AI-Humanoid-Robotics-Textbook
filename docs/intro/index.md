# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Interactive Textbook. This textbook bridges the gap between digital AI and physical embodied intelligence.

## About this Textbook

This interactive textbook is designed to provide hands-on learning experiences with the most important tools and frameworks in Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics:

- **ROS 2**: Robot Operating System 2 for robot software development
- **Gazebo/Unity**: Simulation environments for robotics testing
- **NVIDIA Isaac**: Perception and navigation frameworks for robots
- **Vision-Language-Action Models**: Understanding modern AI approaches to robotics
